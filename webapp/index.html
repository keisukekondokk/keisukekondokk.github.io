Shiny App

COVID-19 Simulator in Japan (beta version)

This shiny app visualizes simulation results of the spatial spread of COVID-19 obtained from the spatial SEIR model with interregional mobility in Japan. See Kondo (2020) for more details.

Kondo, K. Simulating the Impacts of Interregional Mobility Restriction on the Spatial Spread of COVID-19 in Japan. Preprint at medRxiv (2020). https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.12.28.20248926

Caution: Preprints are preliminary reports of work that have not been certified by peer review. They should not be relied on to guide clinical practice or health-related behavior and should not be reported in news media as established information. (from medRxiv)

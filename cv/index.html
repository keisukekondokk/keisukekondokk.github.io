Short Summary

Keisuke Kondo is a Senior Fellow at the Research Institute of Economy, Trade and Industry (RIETI) in Japan.

He holds a Ph.D. in economics from Kobe University, Japan. His research interests span across a wide variety of empirical topics on spatial economics, such as agglomeration economy, spatial spillover effects, local labour markets, interregional labour migration, and international trade.
